TONX Reports Soaring 2025 Revenue, But Toncoin Losses Lead to $148M Net Loss

TONX has released its financial results for 2025, showing a significant jump in revenue. However, these strong gains were overshadowed by losses tied to the company’s holdings in Toncoin, resulting in a net loss for the year.

Although the company did not disclose specific revenue figures, the report indicates that overall earnings expanded considerably compared to previous years. This growth highlights TONX’s ability to boost its income during the period.

Despite the positive revenue performance, the company recorded a substantial net loss of $148 million. The primary reason was large unrealized losses related to Toncoin, the cryptocurrency linked to The Open Network. Unrealized losses represent a decrease in the value of assets the company continues to hold and has not sold.

The report did not include a detailed breakdown of the size or timing of the Toncoin holdings, nor did it clarify exactly how market fluctuations impacted TONX’s balance sheet for the year. Information about other operational, investment, or strategic changes at TONX in 2025 was also not confirmed.

These financial results indicate that while TONX continues to strengthen its revenue base, the volatility surrounding cryptocurrency holdings remains a significant challenge. Additional information may become available from the company in future financial statements.

No leadership commentary or updates about the company’s future outlook were available at the time of writing.
