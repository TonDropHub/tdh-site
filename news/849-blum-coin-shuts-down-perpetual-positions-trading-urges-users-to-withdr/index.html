Blum Coin Shuts Down Perpetual Positions Trading, Urges Users to Withdraw

Blum Coin has announced that its perpetual positions trading service will be discontinued by the end of today. Users with active positions on the platform are being encouraged to withdraw their funds as soon as possible.

This decision marks an end to the perpetual futures trading that has been available to Blum Coin holders. The company has not given any reasons for shutting down the service, nor has it indicated whether this move is temporary or permanent.

Currently, it remains unclear how long users will continue to have access to the withdrawal function or if there are any specific procedures they need to follow to retrieve their funds. Anyone trading or holding positions is advised to act swiftly to avoid potential complications.

No further information has been provided regarding compensation, changes to the platform, or the broader implications for users. Additional updates may be released as the situation develops.

The main message so far has been the urgency for users to promptly manage their holdings, as perpetual positions trading on Blum Coin ends today.
