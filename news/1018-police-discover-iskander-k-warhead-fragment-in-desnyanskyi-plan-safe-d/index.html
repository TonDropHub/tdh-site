Police Discover Iskander-K Warhead Fragment in Desnyanskyi, Plan Safe Detonation

Authorities in Desnyanskyi have reported finding a fragment from an Iskander-K missile warhead. Law enforcement teams identified the fragment and have confirmed that it will be safely neutralized through a controlled detonation.

Residents in the area have been notified while police continue to secure the scene. Officials have emphasized that safety measures are being implemented to eliminate any risk to the public as disposal procedures move forward.

The Iskander-K missile is a type of cruise missile recognized for its powerful destructive capabilities. The discovery of a warhead fragment prompted a swift response from emergency specialists and local authorities.

No additional details have been released regarding how the fragment was found or any potential dangers it might pose to nearby residents. Officials have also not disclosed the specific timing or exact location of the planned detonation.

Authorities are urging local residents to stay calm and follow any instructions or alerts issued by officials. Further updates are expected as the situation develops and once the area is declared safe.
