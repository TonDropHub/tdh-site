TON Foundation Introduces TON Pay SDK for Crypto Payments The TON Foundation has

Released

a new software development kit (SDK) created to simplify crypto payments for developers and businesses. Called TON Pay, this toolkit is designed to make it easier to integrate payment features into apps and services built on the TON blockchain. With TON Pay, developers can more efficiently add cryptocurrency functions—like sending and receiving payments—to their platforms. The launch aims to streamline payment experiences and add more practical value to projects within the TON ecosystem. While details about specific partners or future collaborations have not been revealed, the SDK is expected to boost utility and adoption across TON-based services.
