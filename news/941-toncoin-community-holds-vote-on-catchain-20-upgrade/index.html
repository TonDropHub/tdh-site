Toncoin Community Holds Vote on Catchain 2.0 Upgrade

The Toncoin network is organizing a voting event focused on the proposed Catchain 2.0 protocol update, scheduled to take place from April 8 to April 9, 2026. This marks a significant step in the project’s ongoing development and governance process.

Catchain, the consensus mechanism at the heart of Toncoin’s blockchain, plays a key role in ensuring security and transaction efficiency. The upcoming vote seeks input from the Toncoin community about a planned enhancement to this protocol with the introduction of version 2.0. Details on the specific upgrades or changes involved with Catchain 2.0 have not been confirmed.

Community participation in the decision-making process remains a hallmark of the Toncoin ecosystem, reflecting a commitment to decentralized governance. Network validators and other stakeholders are invited to express their stance on adopting the new version during the voting window. The outcome may determine the direction of future network operations, but specifics about the potential impacts or implementation timeline have not been disclosed.

As of now, it is not clear what modifications are being proposed with Catchain 2.0, nor how these might influence transaction speeds, scalability, or security within Toncoin’s infrastructure.

Participants and observers are encouraged to monitor further announcements for additional information as the voting period unfolds.
