TON Pay Brings Seamless Crypto Payments to Mini Apps TON Pay has introduced a new feature that allows users to

make crypto payments directly within Mini Apps. This update allows Mini App creators to provide smoother transactions, eliminating the hassle of switching between apps or copying payment details. The new tool is expected to encourage broader use of crypto payments for everyday activities within these apps. It's important to verify official sources before making any payments or sharing wallet details when using apps that offer new payment features.
