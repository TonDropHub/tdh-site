TON Wallet Adds Yield Feature for Bitcoin, Ethereum, and USDT

TON Wallet, the crypto wallet platform associated with the TON ecosystem, has launched a yield service for its users. Holders of Bitcoin, Ethereum, and Tether (USDT) can now earn annual yields reportedly as high as 18% directly through the wallet.

This new feature aims to make earning passive returns on digital assets more accessible, allowing users to deposit their chosen cryptocurrencies and start earning yields right from the TON Wallet interface. According to available information, there is no need for external protocols or complex setups to participate.

Specific details about how the yield mechanism operates—whether it relies on staking, lending, or other DeFi strategies—have yet to be disclosed. Information regarding risk levels, lock-up periods, and participation requirements was also not available at the time of writing.

Despite these unanswered questions, this development represents a significant expansion for TON Wallet, which has previously focused on digital asset storage and transfers. The addition of a yield feature could be particularly appealing to users who want to grow their holdings while managing everything from one platform.

As with any yield-generating product, users are encouraged to research further and review all terms of service before participating. Additional information about yield calculations and any limitations is expected to be released as TON Wallet continues to introduce this new feature.
