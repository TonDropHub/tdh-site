TON Wallet Adds Wrapped Bitcoin and Ethereum Tokens TON Wallet has expanded its

Offerings

by introducing new wrapped tokens, wETH and cbBTC, which represent Ethereum and Bitcoin on its platform. These wrapped assets are designed to make it easier for users to access and use major cryptocurrencies within the TON ecosystem, supporting a wider range of use cases and cross-chain activity. With this update, TON Wallet users can now hold and transfer representations of Bitcoin or Ethereum without leaving the network, making cross-chain movement and participation in DeFi more straightforward. Specific details about the integration process and technical requirements have not been disclosed. As always, make sure to double-check addresses and exercise caution when managing new tokens or making transfers.
