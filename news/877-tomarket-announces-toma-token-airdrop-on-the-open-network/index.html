Tomarket Announces TOMA Token Airdrop on The Open Network

Tomarket has revealed the launch of an airdrop campaign for its TOMA token, selecting The Open Network (TON) as the hosting blockchain. This initiative aims to introduce the TOMA token to the TON ecosystem and offer new users an opportunity to engage with the platform.

The campaign’s goal is to increase visibility for TOMA, although details regarding eligibility, campaign duration, and distribution methods have yet to be confirmed. Anyone interested in participating is encouraged to keep an eye on official Tomarket channels for future updates.

The introduction of TOMA to TON reflects a growing trend among projects leveraging established networks to expand their user base and boost engagement. Airdrops like this are increasingly used to generate early momentum and build active communities around new tokens.

At present, further details about the TOMA token’s potential uses, distribution criteria, and any specific requirements have not been disclosed. Participants are advised to exercise caution and wait for more comprehensive information directly from Tomarket.

We will share updates as soon as additional information becomes available. At this time, only the launch of the airdrop has been confirmed, with no further specifics released.
