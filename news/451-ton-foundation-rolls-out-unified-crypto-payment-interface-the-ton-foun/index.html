TON Foundation Rolls Out Unified Crypto Payment Interface The TON Foundation has

introduced a new unified payment interface aimed at making cryptocurrencies easier to use for everyone. This initiative is focused on streamlining the process of making crypto payments, helping to create a more seamless and reliable user experience. By rolling out this solution, the Foundation hopes to simplify digital transactions and open up more ways for people to use crypto in their daily lives. Initial feedback suggests that the new interface brings together a range of payment tools within a single platform, making things less complicated for both senders and recipients. However, further details on which cryptocurrencies will be supported and how the integration will work are still to be confirmed. Anyone interested in exploring new payment methods or tools should remember to use only official resources and take steps to protect their account information.
