About TON Drop Hub

TON Drop Hub tracks public TON ecosystem updates and turns them into short, actionable posts: what happened, why it matters, what to do next, and what risks to watch.

What we publish

Short TON news, airdrop and quest updates, practical guides, and project pages with useful links.

Start with News. Later, the site will also expand Guides, Projects, and Airdrops & Quests.

How to use the site

Read the summary, open the source, verify the details, and never act blindly on hype.

Each post is meant to save time: you get the signal, the link, and the next action without reading dozens of scattered announcements.
